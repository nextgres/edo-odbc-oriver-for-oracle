About EDO

This is the documentation for the EDO driver. EDO is an ODBC driver for the Oracle 9i and above databases. EDO is an acronym for "EDO's Driver for Oracle".

Edo just happens to be my first name. It also is the former capital of Nikkon (Japan). And that's why I have chosen to take the castle of Edo as the central logo for this product.

Building the EDO driver in the first place came from the various bugs and quirks existing in the current official ODBC driver implementation of Oracle itself.
